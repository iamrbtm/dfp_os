## Markets
- **Post-Market Follow-Up Queue**
  After a market, create follow-ups: custom leads, people who asked for a color, unpaid deposits, pickup reminders, and thank-you messages.
- **Market Application Tracker**
  Track markets you might apply to, deadlines, fees, required documents, application status, booth rules, and “worth applying again” notes. This turns market discovery into a pipeline.
- **Table Layout Planner**
  A simple drag-and-drop booth/table layout planner: trays, signs, premium items, impulse items, checkout spot. After a market, record what worked. Over time, correlate layout with sales.
- **Impulse Tray Optimizer**
  A specialized market planning tool for low-cost impulse items. It optimizes for fast decisions, low price points, color variety, and high profit per print hour.

## Promotion
- **Social Content Queue**
  Turn products, market appearances, custom orders, and behind-the-scenes photos into draft post ideas. Human-written or AI-assisted later.
- **Market Display Sign Generator**
  Generate printable product signs with price, QR code, care notes, and short customer-friendly descriptions.

## Report Studio
A place the owner can go to get all the reports in one place.
- **Vendor Market Heat Map**
  Map markets by profit, distance, booth fee, weather risk, traffic, and repeat quality. Helps decide where to apply next.
- **Market Application Tracker Report**
  Working with Market Application Tracker.  This is a report of the same thing

## Products
- **Product Retirement Workflow**
  Instead of simply archiving a product, DFPos walks through: discount remaining stock, hide from public site, remove from POS, preserve historical analytics, mark model/license status, and block accidental reprint.
- **Dead Stock Rescue**
  A workflow for inventory that is sitting too long
- **Product Story Cards**
  For each public product, generate/admin-manage a short “story card”.  These could display as a popup or modal on the public page, kinda like a more details type page.
- **Product Readiness Score**
  Every product gets a 0-100 readiness score based on photos, cost snapshot, license status, inventory, POS visibility, public description, model analysis, price, and sales history. Great for finding half-finished products.
- **Product Photo Shot List**
  For each product missing good photos, generate a shot checklist: hero, scale-in-hand, color variants, close-up, packaging, booth display. Could connect to product readiness scoring.
- **Product Launch Checklist**
  For a new product: license verified, model analyzed, cost snapshot created, photos uploaded, POS tile ready, public description written, inventory target set, market test planned. Prevents half-launched products.

## Printers
- **Printer Maintenance Autopsy**
  When a print fails, DFPos asks 3-5 quick questions: spaghetti, adhesion, clog, layer shift, support failure, filament issue. Over time it builds failure patterns by printer, model, filament, and slicer profile.

## Orders / Custom Orders
- **Local Pickup Scheduler**
  Let customers choose pickup at a market, porch/local pickup window, or arranged handoff. Internally it creates prep tasks and keeps completed orders from being forgotten.

## Booth Mode
A market-day companion screen separate from POS that shows only what matters during an event
- **Booth Break-Even Timer**
  During markets, show: “You need $83 more to cover booth/travel costs.” After break-even, switch to profit tracking.
