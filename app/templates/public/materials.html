What we print with

Materials & options

Every material has its own look, feel, and best use. Here's what we offer and how to choose.

PLA — the everyday choice

PLA (polylactic acid) is our go-to material. It's made from renewable resources, prints cleanly, and comes in hundreds of colors. Most of our dragons, fidgets, flexi animals, and desk items are printed in PLA. It's lightweight, durable for indoor use, and has a smooth matte finish.

Silk PLA — shiny and smooth

Silk PLA has a glossy, almost satin-like finish that makes details pop. It's great for decorative pieces, gifts, and items where appearance matters most. Colors include gold, copper, silver, and jewel tones.

Rainbow & color-shift PLA

Rainbow filament transitions through multiple colors as it prints, creating unique gradients on every item. Color-shift filaments change appearance depending on lighting and viewing angle. Because each print captures the transition at a different point, every piece is one of a kind.

PETG — tough and outdoor-ready

PETG is stronger and more heat-resistant than PLA. It handles sunlight, humidity, and outdoor conditions much better. Choose PETG for signs, business card holders, planters, light manufacturing parts, or anything that will live outside or in a hot car.

Multicolor prints

With our AMS (Automatic Material System) units, we can print items in multiple colors on the same layer. This lets us add text, logos, and details without painting or gluing. Multicolor printing takes longer and uses more material, so it's best for signs, ornaments, and personalized gifts.

Matte & specialty PLA

Matte PLA reduces visible layer lines for a soft, premium look. Glow-in-the-dark, glitter, marble, wood-fill, and carbon-fiber blends are also available for special projects. These specialty materials may affect print speed and pricing — just ask if you have something specific in mind.

Which material should I choose?

Use case Recommended material
Desktop toys, gifts, decor PLA or Silk PLA
Outdoor signs, practical parts PETG
Personalized gifts with text Multicolor PLA / PETG
Unique, one-of-a-kind items Rainbow or color-shift PLA
Vendor or business displays PETG or Matte PLA
Light manufacturing parts PETG

Colors & finishes

We stock a rotating selection of filaments in standard colors, pastels, neons, metallics, and earth tones. If you need a specific color for a custom order, let us know and we'll source it if available. Keep in mind that colors can vary slightly between filament brands and batches.

Most items can be finished with light sanding, but because 3D printed objects have thin walls, aggressive sanding can damage details. For a polished look on certain materials, we can apply clear coat or light acetone vapor treatment for PETG.

Not sure what you need?

If you're unsure which material or finish is right for your project, send us a message. We're happy to walk through the options and recommend what works best for your specific use case.
