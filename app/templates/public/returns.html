Customer support

Returns, replacements, and order help

If something arrives damaged

Please contact us within 7 days of delivery or pickup. Include your order number and a quick photo if possible, and we’ll help with a replacement or solution.

If your order has a print defect

We stand behind our work. If there’s a functional problem caused by the print itself, reach out and we’ll make it right.

Custom-order refund policy

Custom quotes and made-for-you pieces are generally non-refundable once production has started, because they are built specifically for your request.

Changed your mind?

For standard ready-made items, contact us as soon as possible and we’ll let you know whether the order has already been packed, shipped, or started for pickup.
