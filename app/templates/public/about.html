About Dude Fish Printing

Family-run 3D printing based in Clarksville, Tennessee.

Dude Fish Printing started as a creative outlet and grew into a family-run business. What began with a single printer has expanded into a small fleet serving customers at vendor markets, through word of mouth, and online.

We specialize in dragons, fidgets, flexi animals, personalized gifts, and Clarksville/Tennessee-themed products. We also help local businesses with custom display products, QR code signs, and practical printed parts.

Our move to Clarksville marks an exciting new chapter. We're building a shop that can serve the community, support military families, and grow with the needs of our customers.

Our Printer Fleet

  • 4 Bambu A1 printers
  • 2 Bambu X1 Carbon printers
  • 2 Bambu P1P printers
  • 4 AMS Lite units + 1 standard AMS for multicolor printing

What We Make

  • Dragons — articulated and display models
  • Fidgets — satisfying prints for all ages
  • Flexi animals — fun, poseable creatures
  • Personalized gifts — name keychains, custom text items
  • Clarksville/Tennessee-themed decor and gifts
  • Small business products — QR signs, card holders, displays
  • Custom orders — your idea, brought to life
